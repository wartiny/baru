Pico Park: Level 1 Puzzles Walkthrough And Guide

 

Level 1-1

The first level (1-1) is the easiest as it’s the first stage of the game.

You basically need to stack on top of each other to allow the player on top to jump to the other side.

 

After jumping, that player has to press on the button to allow the platform to elongate.

 

This will allow the remaining players to go through.

Use the lift to go up to the door.

Level 1-2

For level 1-2, you should push the wall to the left and grab the key first.

Then, push the wall to the right.

 

Then, the wall (pillar) on the right will fall to the hole and enables you to go through.

For the next part, you push the block to the other end.

Then, the player on the right has to push the block to the left to allow the others to climb over.

 

Head to the exit together.

 

Level 1-3

For level 1-3, one player has to use the staircase to go up while the other waits down here.

 

The player above will push the button to allow the player(s) at the bottom to use the platform.

 

Next, the player at the bottom has to push all the boxes on the red buttons.

This is to remove the blocks in front of the top player.

You can now exit together.

 

Level 1-4

For level 1-4, use the moving platform to climb up.

 

Some of the players have to use the platform in the middle and it requires a certain number of players to go down.

Use the middle platform as a way to hide from the moving pillar.

 

Remember to grab the key at the bottom before leaving the area towards the right.

Finally, use the moving platform to go up. You may need to take turns as the platform may not take all the players at one time.
