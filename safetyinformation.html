Safety Information

  • Please do not use device during charging. Always disconnect the device from  charger before use.
  • Do not use the device if the screen is cracked or broken. Cracked or broken  screen could cause injury to hands or face.
  • Avoid using device and batteries in extreme temperature, such as excessively cold, excessively hot, too humid or too dusty. Your device contains electronics parts and circuits, keep them out of the reach of small children.
  • Switch off your device in any area with a potentially explosive atmosphere.
