Npcs

After adding some props into the room you should add some Npcs into the room as well…
 
 
Since I have 2 chairs in this room, I will add 2 Npcs into the room!
 
 
Now spawn the Npcs into the room.
 
 
While you do this unpause time and place the npcs on top of the chairs…

After that hold the left-mouse button and highlight both of them.
 
 
Then right click and scroll down and then you should see a button called sit.

Click the button and it should position them into a sitting position

After that freeze both of them and proceed to Section 4…
